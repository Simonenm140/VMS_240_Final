Off-campus UNC-CH students often face burdens to composting, and community gardens often need food scraps. CompostMates meets these needs and creates a new resource for our Chapel Hill community.

Nutritious food for all!

CompostMates enriches hundreds of pounds of fresh, organic produce. With the help of our food scraps, our community partners distribute over 4000 pounds of food to hundreds of people in our town each year.


What we do.

Through the coordination of over 20 volunteers, 40 member houses, and 2 community gardens, we work to expand access to composting among off-campus UNC-CH students through a free food scrap pick up service.

Reduce Greenhouse Gases.

In addition to this local impact, CompostMates achieves lower greenhouse gas production in landfills through the diversion of organic waste. We have saved over a ton of (GHGs) from the landfill since 2021!

We strive for shared responsibility for the climate crisis with the larger Chapel Hill community. By strengthening the connections between UNC students and our community, we continuously realize this vision.
